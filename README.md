# Excel_Adidas_Sales_Dashboard
This report summarizes the analysis performed on the Adidas US Sales dataset using Microsoft Excel. The goal was to explore sales performance across regions, retailers, product categories, and sales methods, and to identify key trends that can support business decision-making.

### Project Overview
● To explore overall sales performance across U.S. regions.
● To identify top-performing products and underperforming categories.
● Comparing revenue and units sold by retailer and sales channel.
● To analyze profit margins and pricing trends.
● To discover seasonal or regional sales patterns.
● To provide business recommendations to improve sales and profitability.

### Dataset Description
Retailer & Retailer ID – Identifies the selling partner responsible for the transaction.
Invoice Date – Date the sale was processed.
Region, State, City – Geographic information for location-based analysis.
Product – Adidas product category purchased.
Price per Unit – Selling price of each product.
Total Sales – Revenue generated.
Operating Profit – Profit after operational costs.
Operating Margins – Profitability ratio
Sales Method – Primary sales channel (e.g., Online, In-store, Outlet)

### Data Cleaning and Prepration
The following steps were done to ensure accuracy:
1. Checked for missing or inconsistent values in product, date, and pricing columns.
2. Date format was standardized for trend analysis.
3. The data was converted into a  Table for easier analysis and dynamic referencing.
4. Year and Month was extracted from Invoice data.
